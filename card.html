Pomeranian Puppy

Pomeranian dogs are affectionately known as Poms or Pom Poms. Typically weigh around 7 to 10 pounds. Socializes with other dogs quickly.

  • Breed: Pomeranian
  • Fur color: White
  • Favorite food: Chicken
  • Favorite hobby: Playing with ball

The Pomeranian dog is a breed of dog of the Spitz type that is named for the Pomerania region in Germany and Poland in Central Europe. Classed as a toy dog breed because of its small size. They are intelligent, highly social and fluffy and adorable.
